Interim & Contracting Services

At Wellesley we provide executive contracting solutions to support the short and long term business requirements of our clients. Our experienced contracting team is actively engaged with a network of highly skilled and experienced contractors who are able to hit the ground running and make an immediate impact in your business. The contract team at Wellesley has a reputation for delivering results.

Our consultants provide contract solutions across all key areas of specialization including Technology, Banking & Finance, Strategy & Corporate Development, Marketing, Administration & Customer Service.

Reasons why businesses choose executive contracting:

  • Greater flexibility and control over headcount, resources and budgets
  • Business continuity during peak times
  • Cover for specialist change projects, transformations and strategy implementation
  • Access to a broad talent pool that may otherwise be hard to attract
  • Enhance an existing skill set or determine the right skill set before making a longer term hire
  • Minimal on-boarding time and ability to adapt to new corporate environments
  • Ease of engaging and managing talent, hours and payments
